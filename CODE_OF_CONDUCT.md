# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions
- Accept feedback gracefully and give it kindly
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users
- Show empathy toward other contributors

**Unacceptable behavior:**

- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Project maintainers will remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code. Repeated or severe violations will result in a ban from the project.

Report issues to [orel@orellius.ai](mailto:orel@orellius.ai). All reports will be reviewed and kept confidential.
